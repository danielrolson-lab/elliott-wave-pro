Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 1, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 26, 2026

Short version: We collect only what is necessary to run the app. We do not sell your data. We do not serve ads. Your trade journal and watchlist are yours.

1. Who We Are

Elliott Wave Pro ("we," "us," "our") is a mobile application available on iOS and Android. For privacy inquiries, contact us at daniel.r.olson@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Data

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed password (managed by Supabase Auth). You may optionally provide a display name. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive only the identifier and email that those platforms choose to share.

2.2 Usage Data (On-Device)

The following data is stored locally on your device using encrypted MMKV storage and is never transmitted unless you explicitly sync it:

  • Watchlist tickers and sort order
  • Theme preference (dark/light/system)
  • Chart configuration (timeframe, overlays)
  • Trade journal entries (synced to your Supabase account)

2.3 Data Synced to Supabase

When you are signed in, the following data is synced to your account in our Supabase database (hosted in the United States):

  • Watchlist (tickers, sort order)
  • Alerts (conditions, delivery channels — no push notification tokens stored server-side)
  • Trade journal entries (ticker, direction, entry/exit prices, notes)
  • Subscription tier (mirrored from RevenueCat)

2.4 Subscription and Payment

In-app purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We never receive or store your payment card details. Subscription status is managed by RevenueCat. RevenueCat receives a pseudonymous user ID; we do not share your email address with RevenueCat.

2.5 Market Data

Real-time and historical market data is fetched from Polygon.io. Your ticker queries are transmitted to Polygon using your device's IP address. We do not log which tickers you look up on our servers.

2.6 AI Commentary

When the wave engine generates a commentary request, the current wave count parameters (ticker, wave label, probability, Fibonacci levels, regime) are sent to our Vercel proxy server, which forwards them to Anthropic's API. No personally identifiable information is included in these requests. We do not store AI commentary responses on our servers; they are displayed in the app and discarded.

2.7 Voice Commands

Voice command processing is performed entirely on-device. Audio is not recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server. Microphone access is used solely for real-time keyword matching.

2.8 Crash Reports and Diagnostics

We do not currently integrate a third-party crash reporting SDK. If the app crashes, no diagnostic data is automatically transmitted.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your session
  • To sync your watchlist, alerts, and trade journal across devices
  • To verify your subscription tier and enforce feature gates
  • To generate AI wave commentary (anonymous parameters only)
  • To respond to your support requests

We do not use your data for advertising, behavioural profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

5. Data Retention

Account data is retained for as long as your account is active. Trade journal entries and alerts are retained until you delete them or close your account. Server logs (if any) are retained for no more than 30 days.

6. Data Security

All data in transit is encrypted with TLS 1.2 or higher. Data at rest in Supabase is encrypted at the storage layer. MMKV storage on your device is encrypted. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plaintext.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your trade journal data
  • Object to any processing you did not authorise

To exercise any of these rights, email daniel.r.olson@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. GDPR (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the rights described above under GDPR. Our lawful basis for processing is:

  • Contract performance — to provide the app features you signed up for
  • Legitimate interest — to maintain security and prevent abuse
  • Consent — for voice microphone access (requested at runtime)

9. CCPA (California Users)

We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information with third parties for cross-context behavioural advertising. California residents have the right to know, delete, and opt out of sale (there is no sale to opt out of).

10. Children

Elliott Wave Pro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes by posting a notice in the app and updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after the effective date of a revised policy constitutes your acceptance.
