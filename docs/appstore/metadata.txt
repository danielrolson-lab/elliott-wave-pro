DESCRIPTION:
Elliott Wave Pro is the only mobile trading app that combines institutional
Elliott Wave analysis with real-time options flow, dark pool prints, and
AI-powered scenario commentary — all in a single, professional-grade platform
built for serious market participants.

The Wave Engine automatically identifies Elliott Wave impulse and corrective
structures using Bayesian probability scoring across eight independent factors:
Fibonacci confluence, volume profile, RSI divergence, MACD momentum,
multi-timeframe alignment, time symmetry, market breadth, and GEX (Gamma
Exposure) regime. Each wave count comes with a probability estimate, four
ranked scenarios, specific price targets, and a stop/invalidation level.

Options professionals get a full options chain with Greeks (Delta, Gamma,
Vanna, Charm), IV surface term structure and skew charts, real-time flow feed
sorted by premium with sweep/block/unusual detection, and an earnings
playbook that computes implied vs historical move, IV crush estimates, and
strategy recommendations for any upcoming earnings event. The GEX overlay
plots dealer gamma exposure levels directly on your chart, showing the Zero
GEX (gamma flip) level, Call Wall, and Put Wall.

For institutional traders, Elliott Wave Pro includes a Level 2 depth ladder
and Time & Sales tape with Lee-Ready aggressor detection, a Cumulative Volume
Delta indicator, a dark pool feed sourced from FINRA OTC prints, market
internals (NYSE TICK, TRIN, A/D line, McClellan Oscillator, breadth),
correlation matrix across your watchlist, and a Wave Scanner that matches
current price action against 2+ years of historical analogs. The Trade
Journal auto-fills entry data from the active chart state and tracks R-multiple
performance with behavioral bias detection. AI scenario commentary from
Claude analyzes each wave count in plain English and updates when probability
shifts more than 5%.
