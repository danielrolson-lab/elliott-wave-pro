## Full Description (4,000 characters)

Elliott Wave Pro brings institutional-grade technical analysis to Android. Whether you trade stocks, options, or ETFs, our Bayesian wave detection engine identifies high-probability Elliott Wave setups across all major timeframes — from 1-minute scalps to weekly swing trades.

**WAVE ANALYSIS**
Our engine scores impulse and corrective wave counts using Bayesian probability. You see up to 4 ranked scenarios simultaneously with posterior probability, confidence intervals, Fibonacci targets, and stop levels — all updated in real time on every price tick.

Multi-timeframe alignment scoring adds conviction to your trade thesis. When your 5-minute count and 1-hour count point the same direction, the engine flags the alignment. When they diverge, you know immediately.

**AI COMMENTARY**
When the primary wave probability shifts significantly, Claude AI generates a plain-English explanation of what the wave engine sees and what it means. No guessing, no jargon.

**OPTIONS INTELLIGENCE**
• Full options chain with Delta, Gamma, Vanna, Charm, IV Rank
• Max Pain and Max Gamma strike badges
• IV surface: term structure and 25-delta skew charts
• Live options flow feed: sweeps, blocks, unusual activity
• GEX levels (Zero GEX, Call Wall, Put Wall) plotted on chart

**MARKET DATA**
• Real-time WebSocket ticks via Polygon.io
• Level 2 depth ladder with imbalance ratio
• Time & Sales tape with Lee-Ready aggressor detection
• Cumulative Volume Delta with divergence detection
• Dark pool (FINRA OTC) prints with wave context annotation

**MARKET INTERNALS**
NYSE TICK, TRIN, Advance/Decline line, McClellan Oscillator, breadth statistics, and percentage of stocks above key moving averages — all in one dashboard with auto-divergence detection.

**TRADE JOURNAL**
Log trades in seconds — the journal auto-fills from your current chart state. Analytics show win rate by wave position, win rate by market regime, R-multiple distribution, and behavioral bias flags. Equity curve and monthly P&L bar charts keep your performance in focus.

**WAVE SCANNER**
Search 2 years of history for setups structurally similar to the current wave count. Forward returns at multiple horizons give you statistical context for what typically comes next.

**SOCIAL SENTIMENT**
StockTwits sentiment overlay shows bullish/bearish percentages with divergence detection. A special warning activates at Wave 5 peaks when sentiment is excessively bullish — a classic contrarian signal.

**VOICE NAVIGATION**
Control the app hands-free with natural voice commands. Change timeframe, toggle overlays, open the options chain, switch themes — all without touching the screen.

**SUBSCRIPTION PLANS**
• Free: 3 watchlist tickers, 2 wave scenarios per ticker
• Pro ($24.99/month or $199/year): unlimited tickers, all 4 scenarios, Wave Scanner, Trade Journal, Market Internals, Dark Pool Feed, Wave Summary Grid, Earnings Volatility Tool, Correlation Matrix
• Elite ($59.99/month or $499/year): everything in Pro plus Setup Replay Mode and Quant API access for algorithmic integration

No ads. No data resale. Your watchlist and trade journal are private.
