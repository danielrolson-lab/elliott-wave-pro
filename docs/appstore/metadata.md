### App Description (4,000 character limit)

Elliott Wave Pro delivers institutional-grade Elliott Wave analysis in the palm of your hand. Built for active traders and market professionals, it combines a Bayesian wave detection engine with real-time options data, market internals, and AI-powered commentary — everything you need to identify high-probability trade setups before they develop.

**WAVE ANALYSIS ENGINE**
The core of Elliott Wave Pro is a pure-TypeScript Bayesian wave engine that scores impulse and corrective wave counts across all major timeframes (1m through 1W). You see up to 4 ranked scenarios simultaneously, each with posterior probability, confidence interval, Fibonacci targets, and stop levels. The engine re-scores on every tick and reorders scenarios in real time as probabilities shift.

Multi-timeframe alignment is automatic: when your 5-minute count and 1-hour count agree, the probability jumps. When they conflict, you see a flag instantly.

**FIBONACCI & GEX OVERLAY**
Retracement and extension levels overlay directly on the chart — no cluttered panels, no manual drawing. Gamma Exposure (GEX) levels (Zero GEX, Call Wall, Put Wall) are computed from the live options chain and plotted as horizontal lines so you can see exactly where dealer hedging creates support and resistance.

**AI COMMENTARY**
When the primary wave count probability shifts more than 5%, Claude AI writes a plain-English explanation directly in the app. No jargon — just a clear description of what the wave engine sees and what it means for your trade.

**OPTIONS INTELLIGENCE**
The full options chain shows Delta, Gamma, Vanna, Charm, IV Rank, Max Pain, and Max Gamma on a single scrollable ladder. The IV surface visualizes term structure (contango vs backwardation) and the 25-delta skew. The live options flow feed surfaces sweeps, blocks, and unusual activity with premium and volume/OI filters.

**MARKET INTERNALS**
NYSE TICK, TRIN, Advance/Decline, McClellan Oscillator, and breadth stats in one dashboard. Divergence flags surface automatically when internals disagree with price.

**TRADE JOURNAL**
Auto-fills from chart state — wave label, regime, entry price — so logging a trade takes seconds. Analytics track R-multiple P&L, win rate by wave position, win rate by regime, and behavioral bias flags (cutting winners early, holding losers long). Equity curve and monthly P&L charts make performance review effortless.

**DARK POOL & CVD**
FINRA OTC prints stream in real time with wave context annotation. Cumulative Volume Delta identifies hidden buying and selling pressure with divergence detection.

**WAVE SCANNER**
Scan 2 years of history for setups that looked exactly like the current wave count. Forward returns at 1d, 3d, 5d, 10d, and 20d give you a data-driven edge on what typically happens next.

**VOICE NAVIGATION**
Navigate the entire app hands-free. "Show 15-minute chart for AAPL," "Hide Fibonacci," "Open options chain" — keyword matching works reliably even in noisy environments.
